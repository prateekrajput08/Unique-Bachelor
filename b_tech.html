B.tech (Bachelor Of Technology)

The B.Tech programme is a four-year full-time programme. It is a typical undergraduate degree given in a variety of areas including computer science, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electronics, and information technology.


  • JEE Main and JEE Advanced are the most popular entrance exams for BTech admissions.
  • For Admission, students must have obtained a 10+2 diploma in mathematics, physics, and chemistry.
  • otherwise, have a three-year degree in any engineering field to be admitted to the second year (Lateral entry)

What is B.tech?

The Btech is a four-year undergraduate program that enables students to prepare for professional careers in engineering and technology. The BTech program prepares its students for a variety of careers ranging from product development, manufacturing and automation, to research and innovation.

Everything You Need To Know About The Btech Degree~ Eligibility/Criteria/The Btech Full Form?

If you're looking for a career in information technology, your best bet is to head straight for B.Tech or Bachelor of Technology. There are several reasons why we recommend this program.

Eligibility / Criteria:

  • JEE Main And JEE Advanced Are The Most Popular Entrance Exams For BTech Admissions.
  • For Admission, Students Must Have Obtained A 10+2 Diploma In Mathematics, Physics, And Chemistry.
  • Matriculation With 60% Marks, Sr. Secondary (10+2) 70% Marks (Aggregate) & Minimum 60% Marks In PCM For A Better College.Computer Science/PCB For Biotechnology Without Gap Between 10th And 12th.
